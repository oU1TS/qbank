About oU1TS Question Bank

A student-driven initiative to help oU1TS University students prepare better for their exams by providing access to past questions.

Our Mission

We believe that every student deserves access to quality study materials. oU1TS Question Bank was created to bridge the gap between students and past exam questions, making it easier for everyone to understand exam patterns, practice effectively, and excel in their academics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is access to oU1TS Question Bank free?

Yes, the platform is 100% free and open to all oU1TS University students.

How can I contribute exam papers?

Click on the "Upload" button at the top header, log in, fill in the exam details and upload the PDF file. Submissions are moderated to ensure accuracy.

Can I access it offline?

Yes, downloaded PDFs are saved directly to your device and can be viewed anywhere, anytime.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, upload files, or fill out our contact form. This may include your name, email, and academic details.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to maintain our platform, process uploaded papers, send notifications about submissions, and reply to support messages.

3. File Submissions

Any PDF exam papers uploaded by registered users will be made publicly available to other visitors of the platform. Please verify that uploads do not contain sensitive personal information before submitting.
